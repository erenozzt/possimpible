#  Privacy Policy for Possimpible

Privacy Policy for Possimpible
Effective date: July 15, 2026
Last updated: July 15, 2026

Possimpible (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides a mobile application that offers one meaningful daily mission (or several, based on your settings), optional proof of completion, streaks, XP, and optional Game Center features.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled when you use Possimpible, how it is used, and your choices. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

Contact: [erenozzt@gmail.com]
Developer: Eren Ozturk 

1. Summary
Most of your Possimpible data stays on your device (missions progress, preferences, proofs you choose to save).
We do not operate a separate analytics or advertising network inside the app.
If you sign in to Apple Game Center, Apple may process Game Center–related data (such as your Game Center identity and scores) under Apple’s policies.
Optional device permissions (camera, microphone, location, Health, motion, notifications) are requested only when a feature needs them.
2. Information We Process
2.1 Data stored on your device
Depending on how you use Possimpible, the app may store locally:

Onboarding and settings (preferred categories, free time, avoidances, missions per day, proof-mode options)
Daily mission assignments, completion status, skips, streaks, XP, coins, trust score
Written proofs and optional media proofs (photos, video, audio) if you enable saving proofs
Mission history and related metadata (for example, timestamps, step counts, coordinates for location proofs)
This on-device data is used to run the app for you. It is not uploaded to our servers because Possimpible does not currently use our own backend for syncing account data.

2.2 Information processed through Apple services
Game Center (optional)
If you authenticate with Game Center, Possimpible may send or sync:

Game Center player identity (a user ID managed by Apple)
Gameplay-related content such as leaderboard scores (for example streak or XP) and achievements
This information is processed by Apple as part of Game Center. See Apple’s privacy information:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Push / local notifications (optional)
With your permission, Possimpible may schedule notifications (for example when a mission unlocks or for streak reminders). Notification delivery uses Apple system services. We do not sell notification data.

System permissions you may grant
Camera / Photos / Microphone: to capture mission proofs; media stays on device unless you share it yourself outside the app
Location: for location-based proofs; coordinates may be stored locally with the proof
Health / Motion: for step- or motion-related proofs; used on device for verification
Notifications: for mission unlock and reminder alerts
You can revoke permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

3. How We Use Information
We use on-device and Apple-service data to:

Provide and personalize daily missions
Verify optional proofs and calculate progress (XP, streaks, trust)
Power Game Center leaderboards/achievements when enabled
Send optional notifications you allow
Improve reliability of the product experience on your device
We do not use your data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

4. Sharing
We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only as follows:

With Apple, when you use Game Center, notifications, or other Apple platform services
When required by law, if we receive a valid legal request
With your direction, if you export or share content yourself (for example saving a photo to Camera Roll and posting it elsewhere)
There is no separate advertising partner SDK in Possimpible.

5. Data Retention and Deletion
Local app data remains on your device until you delete it in-app (where available, such as deleting saved proofs) or uninstall Possimpible
Uninstalling the app typically removes local app data from that device
Game Center data retained by Apple is governed by Apple’s policies and your Apple ID / Game Center settings
If you need help with a privacy request, contact [privacy@yourdomain.com].

6. Children
Possimpible is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through our own servers. The App Store age rating and parental controls on the device may further limit access.

7. Security
We rely on iOS sandboxing and on-device storage protections for local data, and on Apple’s security for Game Center and system services. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. International Users
If you use Apple services (such as Game Center), Apple may process data in various countries as described in Apple’s privacy policy.

9. Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use of Possimpible after an update means you accept the revised policy.
